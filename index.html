1970 dodge challenger

Dodge previewed their first model of the Challenger in the fall of 1969. It became an instant hit with car fans. When you look at its body, you can see why. The smooth body contours, the attention-stealing grill and the racing stripes gave the Challenger an identity as a car that looks as good as it drives. The car also came with nine powertrain choices thereby giving drivers unparalleled variety.
